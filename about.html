Our Story

CARVAN started in 2010 with a simple mission: to make car buying easy, transparent, and enjoyable for everyone in India.

We are India's largest online car marketplace, connecting buyers with trusted sellers. With over 5000+ cars and 50+ brands, we've helped 1 lakh+ customers find their perfect vehicle.

Our Journey

2010 - Foundation

CARVAN founded with 10 employees and 100 cars

2015 - Expansion

Expanded to 50 cities across India

2020 - Digital Platform

Launched complete digital car buying experience

2024 - Electric Vehicle Division

Launching dedicated EV section with 20+ models

2025 - International Expansion

Planning to expand to Southeast Asian markets
